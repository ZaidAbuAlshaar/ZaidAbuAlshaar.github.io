Nutrition & Wellness Tips

Evidence-based nutrition advice and lifestyle tips to support your health and fitness goals

Balanced Macronutrients

  • • Protein (25%): Essential for muscle repair and growth. Include lean meats, fish, eggs, legumes, and dairy.
  • • Carbohydrates (45%): Your body's primary energy source. Choose complex carbs like whole grains, fruits, and vegetables.
  • • Healthy Fats (30%): Important for hormone production and nutrient absorption. Include nuts, avocados, olive oil, and fatty fish.

Hydration Guidelines

  • • Drink at least 8-10 glasses (2-2.5L) of water daily
  • • Increase intake during exercise and hot weather
  • • Monitor urine color - pale yellow indicates good hydration

Meal Timing

  • • Eat regular meals every 3-4 hours to maintain energy levels
  • • Have a balanced breakfast within 1-2 hours of waking
  • • Stop eating 2-3 hours before bedtime for better sleep

Exercise & Nutrition

  • • Pre-workout: Light snack with carbs and minimal protein 30-60 minutes before
  • • Post-workout: Protein and carbs within 30-60 minutes after exercise
  • • Stay hydrated before, during, and after workouts
